History

In the depths of a valley, surrounded by beautiful mountains, where rivers roar with uncontrollable force, lies RAFTURA, a rafting company founded by individuals passionate about nature and the spirit of adventure. Our company's motto, "Sail the Adventure, Conquer the Wild," is not just a phrase; it's the guiding principle of our expeditions whose history is intertwined with that of the river that feeds it, the Storm River. This river, with its crystal-clear waters cascading from the mountain heights, is both the muse and the challenge for rafting experts. Every trip with Raftura is an opportunity to embrace the wild beauty of the natural world while facing the controlled fury of the waters. The stories that emerge from journeys with Raftura are as diverse as the people who experience them. From close encounters with wildlife to moments of camaraderie around the campfire at night, each day on the river leaves an indelible mark on the hearts of those who venture with Raftura. With over two decades of experience in the world of rafting, Raftura has forged its reputation as an undisputed leader in the industry. Over the years, they have perfected their craft, combining expert knowledge of the river with an unwavering commitment to the safety and fun of their clients. The Raftura team is made up of skilled individuals whose knowledge of the river is unparalleled. They have navigated every bend and whirlpool of the Storm River and are committed to ensuring the safety and enjoyment of every traveler. At Raftura, adventure awaits those brave enough to navigate the turbulent waters of the Storm River. Are you ready to join the expedition?
